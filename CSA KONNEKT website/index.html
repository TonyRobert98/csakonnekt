About This Site

This is a social network site developed by two CSA (College Saint Andre) students to make it easy for communication of Students and educators in our school College Saint Andrew.

What can this site do?

  • You can chat with your collegues,post something and make your own talent known.
  • You can meet teachers and ask them some questions you have not understood,ask for guidance and chat.
  • Teachers can send works to students during holidays and any class hour.
  • Even Students can help each other in subjects they didn't get well.
